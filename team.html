SLU's Volley Ball Team

SLU’s men’s club volleyball team travels in the fall and spring and competes in the Midwest Plains Volleyball Conference and at the National Collegiate Volleyball Federation nationals.SLU Men's Club Volleyball has competitive teams (A and B) that play over the entire academic year. We hold tryouts early in the fall semester and end with Nationals in April. We are members of the National Collegiate Club Volleyball Federation (NCVF), and travel to several tournaments each semester, in addition to hosting our own. In 2017 and 2019 SLU Men's A team won nationals, and in 2018 took 3rd. We are always looking for fun individuals to support our winning tradition. If you have any questions about the club, no matter your skill level, email us at the address below, or hit us up on Facebook/Instagram. God Bless Go Bills!

SLU’s women’s volleyball competitive club team holds tryouts in the fall and travels to compete in tournaments as well as National Collegiate Volleyball Federation nationals each year.SLU Women’s Club Volleyball is a competitive team that plays all academic year. We hold tryouts in September and end with Nationals in April. We are members of the National Collegiate Club Volleyball Federation (NCVF), Division 1. Beginning in October, we travel to several tournaments each semester and host the Billiken Bash right on SLU's campus. For several years SLU has spent the season in the top 25 ranking list published by NCVF, and has finished strong at Nationals. This year SLU is looking for some new talent. We also have the addition of a B team that is expected to be very competitive. This season SLU is expecting to be a dominating force in the Midwest and on the national level.
